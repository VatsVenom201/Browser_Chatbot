# Privacy Policy for SiteSense

**Last Updated:** March 18, 2026

SiteSense ("we", "our", or "us") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your data when you use the SiteSense browser extension.

## 1\. Information We Do Not Collect

We believe in your privacy. The SiteSense extension **does not** collect, store, or transmit any personally identifiable information (PII). We do not track your browsing history in the background, nor do we sell your data to third parties.

## 2\. How the Extension Works

SiteSense operates by analyzing the content of the specific webpage you are currently viewing **only when you explicitly request it** (e.g., by clicking "Analyze Page" or "Summarize").

* **Webpage Content:** When you analyze a page, the text content of that specific tab is sent to our secure backend server to generate answers and summaries via AI. This data is used temporarily to provide the requested response and is **not** permanently stored, logged, or linked to your identity.
* **Chat History:** Your conversation with the AI is stored temporarily and is not used to identify you.

## 3\. Third-Party Services

SiteSense utilizes secure third-party AI APIs (such as Groq) to process the text and generate responses. We only send the raw text of the webpage and your specific questions to these services. No user-identifying data is included in these requests.

## 4\. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If we make material changes, we will notify you by updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy.
